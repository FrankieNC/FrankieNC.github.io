Welcome to my website! I graduated with First Class Honours in Mathematics from King's College London and am currently pursuing a Master's in Pure Mathematics at Imperial College London. Maths Notes

Here, you will find a collection of my undergraduate university notes organised by academic year. These notes cover a variety of topics and are available in both PDF and LaTeX (.tex) formats for download. Please note: These notes were not originally created for public sharing, so they may contain typos and are not designed as comprehensive learning resources. They are best suited for those who have already taken courses in these subjects and are looking for supplementary material.

Research Interests

My undergraduate thesis focused on geometric and combinatorial group theory, a fascinating branch of mathematics that I encountered for the first time during this project. I developed a deep passion for the subject and am eager to explore it further.

Undergraduate thesis

Under the supervision of Dr. David Sheard, I researched the word and conjugacy problems of groups introduced by Max Dehn in 1911. You can view my thesis here (PDF).
